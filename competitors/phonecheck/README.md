### Most Likely Approaches (informed inference, NOT confirmed)

1. **Linear mover with cameras above AND below** — phone slides on a track, both sides captured without flipping (matches OptoFidelity SCORE pattern)
2. **Transparent platform** — cameras shoot through glass from below for the back
3. **Small clamping gripper that rotates 180°** — not a full robot arm, just a flip mechanism

- Cabinet approach means controlled lighting environment (good for consistency)
